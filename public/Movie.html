The Best Movies According to Me

My top 3 movies of all-times

The Shawshank Redemption

Wrongfully imprisoned banker Andy befriends Red, survives cruelty, secretly tunnels out, exposes corruption,
finds freedom, inspiring hope, perseverance, friendship, justice, redemption, and triumph over adversity inside
Shawshank prison.

Vertigo

Detective with vertigo investigates mysterious woman, obsession grows, discovers deception, love turns tragic.

The Silence in The Lamp

Young FBI trainee interviews cannibal killer to catch another murderer, facing manipulation, fear, psychological terror.

If anyone gets time, I recommend watching these movies- I hope you won't be disappointed.
